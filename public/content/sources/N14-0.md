You’re not lazy, you’re burnt out—

Here’s how to tell:

Burnout doesn’t happen overnight.

It happens in small steps.

At first, it just feels like stress.

Then, it becomes something more.

Here’s what to look for:

🔴 Drowning in Work
🔴 Running on Empty
🔴 Pulling Away from People
🔴 Losing Patience, Losing Focus
🔴 Nothing Feels Worth It Anymore
🔴 Second-Guessing Everything
🔴 Feeling Stuck & Stressed
🔴 Full Burnout Mode

Then, one day—

Even the smallest tasks feel impossible.

That’s full burnout.

But here’s the good news:

You don’t have to get there.

✅ Take real breaks.
✅ Set clear priorities.
✅ Ask for help before it’s too late.
✅ Push back on unrealistic workloads.

Burnout isn’t a sign of hard work.

It’s a sign that something needs to change.

Work should challenge you—
but it shouldn’t break you.

Your energy matters.

Your well-being matters.

You matter.
