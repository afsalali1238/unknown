Top performers don’t think like everyone else—

They do this:

Most people make choices based
on habits, assumptions, or pressure.

But Top Performers?

They use simple methods to make better choices.

Here’s how they do it:

▶️ The First-Principles Method
➜ Break problems into small facts.
➜ Remove ideas that may not be true.
➜ Build solutions from what is real.

▶️ The Eisenhower Matrix
➜ Sort tasks by urgent vs. important.
➜ Focus on what moves things forward.
➜ Stop wasting time on less important tasks.

▶️ The OODA Loop
➜ Observe → Analyze → Decide → Act.
➜ Make fast, smart choices in changing situations.
➜ Adjust quickly when things shift.

▶️ The 5 Whys Method
➜ Ask “Why?” until you reach the real problem.
➜ Solve the cause, not just the symptoms.
➜ Fix issues before they happen again.

The best choices aren’t guesses—
they are clear, structured, and repeatable.

When you change how you think,
you change what is possible.

Better thinking leads to better actions.

Better actions lead to better results.
