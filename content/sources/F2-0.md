Related Work

Other related systems and papers (in order of publication date)
are:


  1974 - 
      "Secure Communications Over Insecure Channels"
      Comms of the ACM 78
      Ralph Merkle
      [Merkle]

      (interactive puzzles based on symmetric ciphers,
      similar to Juels & Brainards Client-Puzzles, but used as a
      building block in Merkle's paper inventing public key
      cryptography)

  1992 - 
      "Pricing via Processing or Combatting Junk Mail",
      CRYPTO 92, Cynthia Dwork and Moni Naor, 
      [Naor]

      (Very similar to hashcash also for store-and-forward
      communications -- was not aware of this publication at time of
      work on hashcash -- cost functions proposed are different)
      
      

   1996 - 
      "PayWord and MicroMint -- Two Simple Micropayment
      Schemes", CryptoBytes, volume 2, number 1, Ron Rivest and
      Adi Shamir, 
      [Rivest]

      (This is a micro-payment system -- and only related in
      that involves hash collisions.)
      

   1996 - 
      "Time-lock puzzles and timed-release Crypto", 
      tech report,
      Ron Rivest, Adi Shamir and David Wagner, 
      [Rivest]

      (This is for time-delayed (artificially CPU expensive)
      decryption, to encrypt messages for the future; tries to be
      non-parallelizable but also mentions a simple parallelizable method
      due to Merkle: to delete some symmetric key bits.)
